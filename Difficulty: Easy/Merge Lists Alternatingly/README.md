<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/merge-list-alternatingly/1">Merge Lists Alternatingly</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Easy</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">Given two linked lists <strong>head1 </strong>and <strong>head2</strong>, insert nodes of the second list into the first list at <strong>alternate </strong>positions. </span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">If all nodes cannot be inserted, keep the remaining nodes unchanged.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">Return an array containing the heads of the modified lists in their original order.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong>head1:<strong> </strong>10 -&gt; 9, head2:<strong> </strong>6 -&gt; 1 -&gt; 2 -&gt; 3 -&gt; 4 -&gt; 5<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928191/Web/Other/blobid0_1778757611.webp" width="181" height="60"> &nbsp;<img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928191/Web/Other/blobid1_1778757626.webp" width="394" height="60"><strong><br></strong><strong>Output: </strong>head1: 10 -&gt; 6 -&gt; 9 -&gt; 1, head2:<strong style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;"> </strong>2 -&gt; 3 -&gt; 4 -&gt; 5<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928191/Web/Other/blobid2_1778757660.webp" width="279" height="60"> &nbsp;<img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928191/Web/Other/blobid3_1778757681.webp" width="279" height="60"><br></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">After inserting nodes of head2 alternately into head1, the modified lists become 10 -&gt; 6 -&gt; 9 -&gt; 1 and 2 -&gt; 3 -&gt; 4 -&gt; 5, as the remaining nodes of head2 cannot be inserted.</span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><br></span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong>head1:<strong> 1</strong> -&gt; 2 -&gt; 3, head2: 4 -&gt; 5 -&gt; 6<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928191/Web/Other/blobid0_1778757988.webp" width="224" height="60"> &nbsp;<img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928191/Web/Other/blobid1_1778758017.webp" width="224" height="60"><br><strong>Output: </strong>head1: 1 -&gt; 4 -&gt; 2 -&gt; 5 -&gt; 3 -&gt; 6, head2: &lt;empty&gt;<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928191/Web/Other/blobid2_1778758046.webp" width="362" height="60"><br><strong style="font-size: 14pt;">Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">After inserting nodes of head2 alternately into head1, the merged list becomes 1 -&gt; 4 -&gt; 2 -&gt; 5 -&gt; 3 -&gt; 6, and head2 becomes empty as all its nodes are used.</span></pre>
